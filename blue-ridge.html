Blue Ridge Mountain

Linville Falls Trail

Located at milepost 316.4, Linville Falls is one of the most iconic waterfalls in the Blue Ridge Mountains. The Erwins View Trail offers multiple viewpoints of the falls and is a moderate 1.6-mile round trip hike.

Craggy Pinnacle Trail

This short but rewarding trail offers a near 360-degree view of the Blue Ridge Mountains. At just 1.4 miles round trip, it’s perfect for novice hikers and provides stunning vistas, especially during early summer blooms.

Boone Fork Trail

A challenging 5.5-mile loop located near Julian Price Park, this trail features diverse terrain, river crossings, and ends at the scenic Boone Fork Falls. It’s a favorite for experienced hikers seeking adventure.
