Moderate Trails

Moore's Wall Loop Trail

Located in Hanging Rock State Park, this 4.3-mile loop trail offers rewarding views at the top. It features rock staircases, forest shade, and a panoramic overlook of the Sauratown Mountains.

Graveyard Fields Loop

A Blue Ridge Parkway favorite, this moderate 3.2-mile loop includes a waterfall, wooden boardwalks, and colorful foliage in the fall. Slight elevation makes it great for those seeking a scenic workout.

Craggy Pinnacle Trail

This 1.4-mile round-trip trail gains just enough elevation to be considered moderate. You'll get sweeping views of the Blue Ridge Mountains from the summit, especially magical during rhododendron bloom season.
